WHO WE ARE

We are believers! We believe in Africa. We believe in Africa's youths.
We believe in Africa's potentials. But we are more than believers. We are doers!





RUDIGO has a mission.

Our mission is to reduce unemployment rates by empowering youths on-demand skills; and connecting them to job oppourtunities within and outside Africa.
Our mission is to breed exceptional technology solution providers within, across, from Africa, while simultaneously using our prepared work-force to solve problems for individual and corporate clients and customers all over the world.





WHAT WE DO




THE OPPOURTUNITY

Cries have been and keep being made, for talent in the Information Technology space. It's like a market that can never be filled! We want to do our part to bridge the talent pool.

OUR APPROACH

We source for young, smart folks with no current engagement, take them off the streets, train and equip them with highly soft-after skills in the information technology space, and expose them to companies, agencies and individuals requiring their services.

OUR MODEL

Our pilot training program is focused on breeding professional web and mobile app developers; and then connecting them to freelance development jobs from home and abroad.
